## About

RIGBY Capital is a European technology asset financing and lifecycle management company, subsidiary of [SCC France](https://france.scc.com/) -- a leader in digital distribution and services. We help **700+ clients** across France and the UK unlock value from their IT, medical, and industrial equipment through tailored leasing, As-a-Service, and fleet management solutions.

**20+ years** of experience &middot; **80 employees** across Europe &middot; **500K+ assets** under management &middot; **500M+ EUR** managed portfolio

### A propos

RIGBY Capital est une entreprise europeenne specialisee dans le financement et la gestion du cycle de vie des actifs technologiques. Filiale de [SCC France](https://france.scc.com/), nous accompagnons plus de 700 clients en France et au Royaume-Uni dans la valorisation de leurs equipements informatiques, medicaux et industriels, a travers des solutions de location sur-mesure, d'As-a-Service et de pilotage de parc.

We develop internal platforms, integrations, and tools that power our asset management operations and client-facing services:
